A creative solution for the modern gift-giving dilemma: how do you make a digital game purchase feel like a proper present?

## The Problem

Digital games are convenient to buy but lack the tactile joy of unwrapping a physical gift. This tool bridges that gap by generating printable gift cards that can be wrapped and given alongside digital game codes.

## Features

- Generate custom gift cards for digital games
- Print-ready output for various game platforms
- Makes digital gifts feel more personal and tangible
